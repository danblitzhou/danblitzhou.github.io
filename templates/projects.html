Oh My Fish

  • Login Page

    Create a login by entering your email and password

  • My Operation Log

    A list of current user's fishing operatinos

  • Nearby Fishery Information

    See the summary of fishery information in a specific location

  • Start a new operation

    Record critical informations of a fishing operation before you start one

Fishhackthon 2016 Toronto entry project; The goal of creating this app is to encourage and help individual fishermen or smaller fishing groups. This app provides a simple yet standardized platform to store and share fishery info. With Oh my fish, individuals or small fishing groups can have a easier way to manage their data; the big data collected by Oh my fish can also enhence fishing efficiency of individual users or provide reference for government and non-profit organizations.

Oh my fish is developed with Ionic/angularJS framework and Firebase
